history

Founded by Hans Wilsdorf in 1905, Rolex quickly established itself as the pioneer of precision watchmaking. From the first waterproof watch, the Oyster, to conquering the depths of the ocean and the peaks of Everest, Rolex has become synonymous with quality and prestige.
